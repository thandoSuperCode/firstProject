ABOUT

An anime character holding a soccer ball in his hand and leaning on the ground to catch it Blue Lock is a sports anime that centers around the intense world of soccer. The series tells the story of a group of young soccer players who have been handpicked to participate in a unique training program called Blue Lock. The program is run by Ego Jinpachi, who intends to "destroy Japanese loser football" by introducing a radical new training regimen. The program isolates 300 young forwards in a prison-like institution called "Blue Lock" and puts them through rigorous training aimed at creating "the world's greatest egotist striker".
